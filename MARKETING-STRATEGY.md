# GraphForge Marketing Strategy

## Product Positioning

**Tagline:** "See your knowledge in a whole new dimension."

**Elevator Pitch:** GraphForge transforms Obsidian's flat note list into an immersive 3D knowledge graph. It's the only true 3D graph visualization plugin for Obsidian, turning your notes into an interactive universe you can explore, analyze, and share.

## Target Market

### Primary: Obsidian Power Users
- 1M+ active Obsidian users
- Knowledge workers, researchers, writers, developers, students
- Already invested in note-taking, willing to pay for productivity tools
- Active in Obsidian Discord, Reddit, forums

### Secondary: Enterprise Knowledge Management
- Teams using Obsidian for internal wikis
- Research labs, consulting firms, legal practices
- Companies spending $10K-100K/year on knowledge management tools

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Weakness | GraphForge Advantage |
|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| Juggl | 2D only, no bloom/glow | True 3D + visual effects |
| Neo4j Graph View | Requires Neo4j database | Works directly with Obsidian vault |
| Breadcrumbs | Hierarchy only, no 3D | Full 3D spatial visualization |
| Obsidian native graph | Basic 2D, no interactivity | Immersive 3D with physics |

## Revenue Model: Freemium

### Free Tier
- Basic 3D graph view
- Up to 200 nodes
- Dark/Light themes
- Basic search & filter

### Pro Tier ($9.99/month or $79.99/year)
- Unlimited nodes
- All 10 features (bloom, animations, clustering, analytics, AI suggestions, temporal mode, export, collaboration, voice commands, QAbot)
- All themes including Neon
- Priority support

### Enterprise Tier ($49/user/month)
- Everything in Pro
- Team collaboration (real-time multi-user)
- Admin dashboard
- SSO integration
- Custom branding
- SLA support

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Community Launch (Month 1-2)
1. Publish to Obsidian Community Plugin Store
2. Create demo video showing 3D graph with time-travel feature
3. Post on r/ObsidianMD, Obsidian Discord, Twitter/X
4. Target: 500 free installs

### Phase 2: Product Hunt Launch (Month 3)
1. Launch on Product Hunt with polished demo
2. Offer 50% discount for early adopters
3. Target: 100 paying customers ($1K MRR)

### Phase 3: Content Marketing (Month 3-6)
1. YouTube tutorials: "How to visualize your knowledge in 3D"
2. Blog posts: "The future of knowledge management"
3. Guest posts on Obsidian community blogs
4. Target: 1,000 free installs, 200 paying ($2K MRR)

### Phase 4: Enterprise Outreach (Month 6-12)
1. Target Obsidian-for-teams use cases
2. Conference talks (Obsidian Camp, knowledge management conferences)
3. Case studies with early enterprise adopters
4. Target: 5 enterprise deals ($100K+ ARR)

## Key Metrics to Track

- Free installs → Pro conversion rate (target: 5%)
- Monthly churn rate (target: <5%)
- Net Promoter Score (target: >50)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (target: <$20)
- Lifetime Value (target: >$200)

## Marketing Channels (Ranked by ROI)

1. **Obsidian Discord & Reddit** -- Free, highly targeted, community-driven
2. **YouTube demos** -- Visual product = perfect for video, viral potential
3. **Twitter/X** -- Build in public, share screenshots, engage with productivity community
4. **Product Hunt** -- One-time launch boost, credibility
5. **SEO/Blog** -- Long-term organic traffic from "obsidian graph" searches
6. **Paid ads** -- Only after product-market fit is proven

## Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

1. **Only true 3D graph in Obsidian** -- No competitor offers this
2. **Time-travel feature** -- Watch your knowledge grow over time (great for demos)
3. **AI-powered suggestions** -- Automatically find missing connections
4. **Export & share** -- Turn your knowledge graph into a shareable artifact
5. **QAbot** -- Natural language queries against your knowledge base

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Obsidian changes plugin API | Stay updated with Obsidian releases, maintain compatibility |
| Competitor clones 3D feature | Move fast, build community moat, add unique features |
| Low conversion rate | Improve onboarding, add free trial of Pro features |
| Performance issues with large vaults | Implement view modes, optimize rendering, add node limits |
